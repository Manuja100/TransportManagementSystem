You will get paid for the amount of trips you complete on a monthly basis. If you have any more questions reguarding this, please refer to our Salary page or contact our finance department by dialing our hotline

How do I complain about a customer ?

As a well reputed company, we always look our for the people who helped us get here. If you are faced with any form of discrimination or hardship from a customer or another driver or a member of staff of our company, please make a complaint to our hotline or send us a msg using your profile
