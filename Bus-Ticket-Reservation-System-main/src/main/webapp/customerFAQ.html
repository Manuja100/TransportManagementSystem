FAQ - Customer

How do I book a seat ?

Click on the register/signup button on the home page and create an account if you have not. Select your route, select your seat and make the payment. Your seat is booked!!!!

How do I complain about a driver ?

Safety and welbeing of our passengers are at our utmost priority. If your are faced with any difficulty with any one of our staff members, please lodge a complaint using our website or call us to our hotline.

How do I edit my personal details ?

One you have logged in to our website. Click on your profile icon and select edit profile. Edit the relevent field and click on make changes.
